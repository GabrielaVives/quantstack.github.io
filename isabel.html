Isabel Paredes
Robotics Software Developer

@IsabelParedes

Isabel Paredes is a full-time software developer at QuantStack. She initially joined QuantStack as an intern where she completed her master thesis in "Cross-compiling ROS 2 to WebAssembly for the Development of a Web Browser Supported Environment." She graduated from RWTH Aachen with a degree in M.Sc. Robotic Systems Engineering.

Before joining QuantStack, she worked as a research assistant for the IGMR at RWTH, where she developed tutorials to introduce students to ROS with the help of Jupyter notebooks.

Currently, she works on the development of Jupyter Xeus and maintains packages on emscripten-forge.
